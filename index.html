The Solution

A Five-Stage AI Reasoning Pipeline

The architecture is deliberately additive. Existing Unity components remain untouched; the veratex intelligence layer sits in the middle and returns verified output to Unity's operator tools.

Vision Analyst

Detects people, vehicles, and objects—the baseline detection layer most AI camera products stop at.

Behavior Analyst

Flags anomalies against site-specific patterns over time, rather than relying on a single frame.

Reasoning Analyst

Creates a plain-language incident narrative so operators receive context, not just another clip.

Synthesis Node

Cross-checks the analysts, weighs confidence, and suppresses false positives through consensus scoring.
